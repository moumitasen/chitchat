Moumita Sen

Moumita is from Kolkata (India) but has been living in San Francisco (USA). She wants to make learning English fun and effective by having face-to-face conversations with real people. Moumita has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a consultant/marketing professional in Singapore, India and USA. An entrepreneur at heart, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry for five years, before sharting Chitchat. Given her strong business background, she focuses on conducting Chitchat's Business English Lessons as well as Job Interview Practice Sessions.

Lisa Baughn

Lisa lives in Colorado (USA) but believes herself to be a citizen of the world. She has traveled to Singapore, Malaysia, China, Nepal, Thailand, Laos, France, Italy, Turkey, Cyprus, Mexico and Canada. She taught high-school English as a Fulbright English Teaching Assistant in Indonesia. She has a Master's degree in Geography as well as a TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) certification from The Language House in Prague. She looks forward to meeting new students and taking upon the challenge of improving their English skills and attaining their learning goals.
