About Us

Moumita Sen

Founder

Moumita started Chitchat in 2012. She is a native English speaker and currently lives in San Francisco, USA. She has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a consultant/marketing professional in Singapore, India and USA. Prior to starting Chitchat, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry export for five years.

Moumita loves having conversations with people from other countries, learning about their culture and sharing her own experiences. And that is what made her start Chitchat. She wants to make learning English fun by having real conversations with people rather than memorizing boring grammar rules.
