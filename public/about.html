About

Moumita Sen

Founder

Moumita started Chitchat in 2011. She is a native English speaker and currently lives in San Francisco, USA. She has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a consultant/marketing professional in Singapore, India and USA. Prior to starting Chitchat, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry export for five years.
Moumita loves having conversations with people from other countries, learning about their culture and sharing her own experiences. And that is what made her start Chitchat. When she is not conducting classes for Chitchat, she is busy creating websites. In fact, the Chitchat website was designed and developed by her.
