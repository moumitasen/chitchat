About Us

Chitchat was started by Moumita in 2012. A friend asked Moumita to help his mother with English conversations and Moumita agreed to do a few lessons. But soon she started getting requests for classes from other non-English speakers travelling to San Francisco. She was really enjoying teaching English and helping others improve their language skills, and that's when she decided to start Chitchat officially!

Currently Chitchat has students across 8 countries - USA, Brazil, Argentina, Mexico, Chile, Japan, Korea and France. Chitchat's classes aim to make students confident English-speakers by providing them with the right tools and personalizing classes to suit their specific needs.
