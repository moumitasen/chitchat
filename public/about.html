Moumita Sen

Founder

Moumita started Chitchat in 2011. She was born and brought up in India and currently lives in San Francisco, USA. She has an MBA specializing in Marketing from Arizona, USA and has worked as a consultant/marketing professional in Singapore, India and USA. Prior to starting Chitchat, she ran her own business in high-end jewelry export for the past 5 years.
She loves teaching English and conducts all the classes on Conversation and Business English.
